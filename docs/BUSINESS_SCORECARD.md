# 📊 Tash8eel Business Scorecard

## Executive Summary

**Tash8eel = WhatsApp Sales Employee for Egyptian SMBs**

Unlike Odoo (back-office ERP), Tash8eel is the front-line sales agent that handles customer conversations, takes orders, manages inventory, and recovers abandoned carts—all through WhatsApp in Egyptian Arabic.

---

## 1. Top 10 Egyptian SMB Pains Tash8eel Solves

| #   | Pain Point               | How Merchants Suffer Today                                   | How Tash8eel Solves It                                               |
| --- | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | **WhatsApp Overwhelm**   | Merchants manually reply to 50-200 messages/day, miss orders | AI handles 80%+ of conversations automatically                       |
| 2   | **COD Risk & No-Shows**  | 15-25% of COD orders fail delivery, money lost               | Address validation + delivery tracking + payment link fallback       |
| 3   | **Egyptian Bargaining**  | Customers haggle, merchants inconsistent                     | Policy-based negotiation with max discount rules                     |
| 4   | **Messy Addresses**      | "Next to the pharmacy" causes failed deliveries              | Structured address depth (city/area/street/building) + location pins |
| 5   | **Voice Note Orders**    | Customers send voice, merchants replay 10x                   | Automatic transcription → structured order extraction                |
| 6   | **Courier Failures**     | No visibility until customer complains                       | Real-time delivery status polling + alerts                           |
| 7   | **Stockouts & Oversell** | Sell item, realize it's out of stock                         | Real-time inventory with reservation + low-stock alerts              |
| 8   | **Abandoned Carts**      | Customer stops replying, order lost                          | Multi-step followup sequences with incentives                        |
| 9   | **No Reporting**         | Merchants guess their numbers                                | Daily/weekly reports via WhatsApp + portal                           |
| 10  | **Manual Everything**    | Staff = expensive, error-prone                               | Automation saves 4-6 hours/day per merchant                          |

## 4. "Merchant NEED" Criteria (Non-Negotiable)

A feature is "merchant-need" only if ALL these are true:

### ✅ Must-Have Criteria

| #   | Criteria             | Test                                                                                      |
| --- | -------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | **No SQL Required**  | Merchant can do everything from Portal + WhatsApp                                         |
| 2   | **Outcome Visible**  | Merchant sees daily/weekly/monthly impact (orders, revenue, time saved)                   |
| 3   | **Instant Takeover** | Merchant can take over any conversation in < 5 seconds                                    |
| 4   | **Stock Awareness**  | Merchant sees low-stock alerts, cannot oversell                                           |
| 5   | **Money Clarity**    | Merchant sees revenue, discounts, pending payments (even before full payment integration) |
| 6   | **Works on Mobile**  | Portal is usable on merchant's phone (60%+ use phones)                                    |
| 7   | **Egyptian Context** | Handles voice notes, location pins, Egyptian Arabic, COD, bargaining                      |

### ❌ Not Merchant-Need (Avoid)

- Features only developers use
- Analytics without actionable insights
- Complex configuration requiring training
- Features that work "sometimes" or "in ideal conditions"
