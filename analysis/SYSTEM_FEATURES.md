# Tash8eel — Full System Feature Reference

**Platform Category:** AI-Powered Arabic WhatsApp Commerce & Operations Platform
**Target Market:** Arabic-speaking merchants (Saudi Arabia, GCC, MENA region)
**Core Value Proposition:** Run your entire business from WhatsApp — orders, inventory, payments, support, marketing, and forecasting — all powered by AI, all in Arabic.

---

## SECTION 1: CORE COMMERCE ENGINE

### 1. WhatsApp-Native Order Management
Customers place orders directly through WhatsApp conversations. The AI agent understands Arabic text, extracts product names, quantities, and delivery details automatically. Orders flow into a full management dashboard with status tracking (pending → confirmed → shipped → delivered). Merchants can view, edit, cancel, and export all orders. Supports multi-item orders, partial refunds, and order notes.

### 2. Smart Conversation Management
Every WhatsApp conversation is captured, threaded, and stored. The AI classifies incoming messages as orders, complaints, inquiries, or payment proofs. Agents can take over from the AI at any time. Conversations are tagged, searchable, and linked to customer profiles and order history. Supports handover protocols, internal notes, and conversation assignment to specific staff.

### 3. Customer Profiles & History
Every customer has an auto-created profile that aggregates their full purchase history, total spent, last order date, average order value, and WhatsApp interactions. Merchants can add tags, notes, block numbers, or flag VIP customers. Search by name, phone, or order ID. Full GDPR-compatible data export.

### 4. Product Catalog Management
Full product catalog with variants (size, color, weight), SKU management, pricing, images, categories, and stock levels. Products are indexed and used by the AI to answer customer questions instantly. Bulk import/export via CSV. Arabic product names and descriptions natively supported.

### 5. Payment Proof Verification
Customers send bank transfer screenshots or payment slips via WhatsApp. The system captures these images, extracts key fields (amount, date, reference number) using OCR, and presents them to the merchant for single-click approval. Approved proofs automatically update order payment status. Full audit trail of all verifications.

### 6. Delivery & Shipping Management
Track orders through the delivery lifecycle. Assign orders to couriers or delivery drivers. Set delivery zones and fees. Track real-time delivery status. Automatic WhatsApp notifications to customers at each stage. Supports both internal delivery teams and third-party couriers. Return and exchange flows included.

### 7. Delivery Driver Portal
Dedicated portal for delivery drivers to view assigned orders, update delivery status, capture proof of delivery (photos, signatures), and report issues. GPS-linked zone assignment. Driver performance dashboard for managers.

## SECTION 2: AI AGENT ENGINE

### 8. Arabic AI Sales Agent
The primary AI agent handles inbound WhatsApp messages 24/7. It understands colloquial Arabic dialects, responds naturally, answers product questions from the catalog, takes orders, confirms prices, applies discount codes, and collects delivery details. Fine-tuned for commerce contexts. Handles ambiguous requests with clarifying questions rather than failing silently.

### 9. AI Support Agent
A dedicated AI mode for after-sales support. Handles complaints, order status inquiries, refund requests, and escalations. Knows the full order history for each customer. Escalates to human agents when emotion detection triggers (frustration, urgency keywords). Logs every escalation with reason.

### 10. AI Collections Agent (Follow-up Engine)
Automatically identifies outstanding invoices and unpaid orders. Sends polite Arabic payment reminders via WhatsApp on a scheduled cadence. Stops when payment is received. Escalates to manager after configurable number of unanswered attempts. Tracks response rates and payment conversion.

### 11. Inventory AI Service
AI-powered inventory insights. Automatically generates Arabic product descriptions from SKU data. Detects duplicate or merged SKUs. Calculates FIFO valuation. Identifies slow-moving and fast-moving products. Suggests reorder quantities based on sales velocity. Detects expiry risk for perishable products.

### 12. AI Daily Brief
Every morning at a configurable time, merchants receive an Arabic WhatsApp summary: yesterday's revenue, top products, pending orders, low-stock alerts, any anomalies or unusual patterns. Generated by AI with trend commentary — not just raw numbers.

### 13. AI Weekly Report
A full business performance report delivered via WhatsApp every Monday morning (or configured day). Covers: sales trend vs prior week, top customers, fulfillment performance, inventory health, and forward-looking action recommendations written in Arabic.

## SECTION 3: INVENTORY & SUPPLY CHAIN

### 14. Multi-Location Inventory
Track stock across multiple warehouses or storage locations. Transfer stock between locations. Set location-specific reorder thresholds. View per-location availability in real time. Full movement log with timestamps and who made each change.

### 15. Inventory Insights Dashboard
Aggregated intelligence on inventory health. Shows fast-movers, slow-movers, overstock, understock, and days-of-supply per product. Charts for stock trends over time. Exportable reports. Arabic product names rendered correctly throughout.

### 16. Expiry Date Alerts
For merchants selling perishable or date-sensitive products. Enter expiry dates per batch/lot. System flags products expiring within a merchant-configurable horizon (e.g., 30 days). Automatic WhatsApp alerts. View and act on an expiry priority list from the dashboard.

### 17. FIFO Inventory Valuation
First-In-First-Out cost calculation for accurate gross margin reporting. Each purchase batch retains its unit cost. Sales are attributed to the oldest batches first. Provides true cost of goods sold (COGS) and current inventory value at cost. Essential for financial reporting.

### 18. SKU Deduplication & Merge Tool
AI detects products that are likely duplicates based on name similarity, barcode, or price. Presents merge candidates to the merchant. One-click merge that consolidates stock quantities, sales history, and customer references. Eliminates catalog fragmentation that inflates apparent SKU count.

### 19. Supplier Management Portal
Maintain a directory of suppliers with contact details, product-to-supplier mappings, lead times, and pricing history. Log purchase orders, delivery receipts, and quality issues. AI can suggest which supplier to reorder from based on price, lead time, and past performance score.

### 20. AI Supplier Discovery
Enter a product type or category and the system automatically searches for potential suppliers (via Google Places API or AI-generated suggestions). Returns names, locations, ratings, and contact information. Saves hours of manual research for new product lines.

### 21. Purchase Orders
Create, send, and track purchase orders to suppliers. Set expected delivery dates. Receive stock against a PO. Track partial receipts. Auto-update inventory levels when PO is received. Full PO history per supplier.

## SECTION 4: AUTOMATION ENGINE

### 22. Low-Stock Auto-Alert
When any product falls below its configured minimum stock threshold, an automated WhatsApp message is sent to the merchant or designated manager. Alert includes product name, current quantity, recommended reorder amount, and a link to the supplier.

### 23. Reorder Reminder Automation
When stock for a linked supplier product reaches the reorder point, the system automatically generates a draft WhatsApp message to the supplier contact requesting restocking. Merchant approves and sends with one tap.

### 24. Payment Follow-up Automation
When an order remains unpaid past a configurable deadline, the system automatically sends a friendly Arabic payment reminder to the customer. Configurable delay, message template, and max number of attempts. Stops automatically on payment.

### 25. Order Milestone Notifications
Automatic WhatsApp notifications to customers when their order moves through key stages: confirmed, preparing, shipped, out for delivery, delivered. All messages in Arabic, branded with the merchant's name. Fully configurable per stage.

### 26. SLA Breach Alert
When a conversation or support ticket exceeds the merchant's configured response SLA (e.g., 4 hours without reply), an internal WhatsApp alert is sent to the assigned agent and their manager. Prevents customers from being forgotten.

### 27. Daily Sales Report Automation
At end of day, the system computes the day's total revenue, order count, average order value, and top products, then delivers a formatted Arabic summary to the merchant via WhatsApp — zero manual effort.

### 28. Customer Win-Back Campaign Trigger
When a previously active customer hasn't ordered in a configurable number of days (e.g., 21 days), the automation sends them a personalised win-back message offering a discount or asking if they need help. Targets lapsed customers before they churn permanently.

### 29. Subscriber Renewal Reminder
For merchants who sell subscriptions or recurring products: automatic reminders are sent to customers a configurable number of days before renewal (e.g., 7 days, 3 days, 1 day). Reduces involuntary churn from forgotten renewals.

### 30. Upsell Trigger After Delivery
After an order is marked delivered, after a configurable delay (e.g., 48 hours), the system sends a WhatsApp follow-up with related product suggestions and an easy reorder link. Drives repeat purchase without any manual outreach.

### 31. Birthday/Anniversary Greeting
When a customer's birthday (or account anniversary) is reached, an automatic personalised WhatsApp message is sent with an optional offer or coupon code. Builds emotional connection and loyalty.

### 32. Flash Sale Announcement Automation
Merchants define a flash sale window (start time, end time, discount). At the start time, the automation broadcasts a WhatsApp message to all eligible customers. Includes countdown language and clear call-to-action.

### 33. Abandoned Cart Recovery
When a customer starts an order flow but doesn't complete it within a configurable window (e.g., 2 hours), the automation sends a WhatsApp nudge with a summary of what they had and an invitation to complete the purchase.

### 34. Negative Review Response Alert
When a customer sends a message containing negative sentiment keywords, an alert is automatically triggered to the manager. Ensures unhappy customers are not missed in a high-volume inbox.

### 35. Inventory Sync Alert (POS)
When inventory levels in a connected POS (point-of-sale) system diverge from Tash8eel's records by more than a configurable threshold, an automatic reconciliation alert is sent. Prevents stockouts caused by out-of-sync systems.

### 36. Automation Control Center
A dedicated merchant portal page where all automation types are listed. Each automation can be independently toggled on/off, with configurable schedules (day of week, time of day), triggers (thresholds, delays), and message templates. No code required — full merchant self-service.

### 37. Real-Time Operations Dashboard
Live overview of today's orders, revenue, new customers, active conversations, and fulfillment rate. Key performance indicators update in real time. Color-coded status cards, trend arrows vs yesterday, and a live activity feed. The merchant's "command centre" for the day.

### 38. KPI Dashboard
Tracks long-term key performance indicators: monthly revenue trend, customer acquisition rate, average order value over time, fulfillment SLA compliance, agent response time, and customer satisfaction proxy. Configurable date ranges. Exportable as PDF or CSV.

### 39. Revenue & Sales Analytics
Drill-down analytics for revenue: by product, by customer segment, by day of week, by delivery zone, by agent. Identify peak hours, best-selling variants, and revenue concentration risk. Compare any two periods side-by-side.

### 40. CFO Summary Report
A financial-grade monthly report formatted for business owners and finance teams. Includes: gross revenue, refunds, net revenue, estimated COGS, gross margin %, top 10 products by margin, and cash flow narrative. Downloadable as PDF.

### 41. Accountant Package Export
Export all transactional data in a structured format compatible with common Arabic accounting systems. Includes itemised sales journal, tax breakdown, refund log, and payment method split. Saves hours of manual bookkeeping.

### 42. Agent Activity Analytics
Per-agent performance dashboards: conversations handled, average response time, resolution rate, escalation rate, and orders converted. Includes a 24-hour message volume heatmap showing when each agent is most active. Useful for scheduling and coaching.

### 43. WhatsApp Delivery Trend Chart
Tracks WhatsApp message delivery rates, read rates, and response rates over time. Identifies if delivery quality is degrading (e.g., number flagged as spam, API failures). Helps merchants monitor communication health.

Send targeted push notifications to customers who have the merchant's mini app or PWA installed. Separate from WhatsApp — an additional channel for re-engagement and announcements.

## SECTION 7: PREDICTIVE FORECASTING PLATFORM (NEW)

### 49. Demand Forecasting with Confidence Bands
Uses Holt-Winters Double Exponential Smoothing on up to 90 days of sales history to predict future sales velocity per SKU. Returns 7-day, 14-day, and 30-day forecasts with 95% confidence interval upper and lower bounds. Detects rising and falling demand trends. Updates nightly automatically.

### 50. Automated Stockout Date Prediction
For every product, the system calculates precisely how many days the current stock will last at the forecasted demand rate, and predicts the exact expected stockout date. Color-coded urgency levels (Critical / High / Medium / Low) ensure merchant attention is directed to the right products first.

### 51. Safety Stock & Reorder Point Engine
Calculates the mathematically optimal safety stock level for each product using demand standard deviation and supplier lead time (Z=1.65 for 95% service level). Computes the exact reorder point so merchants know when to order, not just when stock runs out.

### 52. Replenishment Recommendations
Generates a prioritised purchase order recommendation list: what to order, how much to order, and when. Merchant can approve with one click, which logs the PO and updates inventory planning. Dismissal and ordered statuses for full workflow tracking.

### 53. Cash-Flow Forecast (30-Day Runway)
Projects daily inflows (expected revenue), outflows (expenses), and net balance for the next 30 days using a rolling average smoothed model. Calculates runway days: how many days until cash balance goes negative if current trends continue. Highlights specific risk dates.

### 54. Customer Churn Prediction
Uses survival analysis (exponential decay model) to calculate the probability that each customer has churned based on days since their last order vs their historical order cycle. Ranks customers by churn probability. Provides an AI-recommended re-engagement action for each at-risk customer.

### 55. SLA Breach Probability Forecast
For every open conversation, predicts the probability it will breach SLA before being resolved, based on how long it has been open vs the merchant's historical average resolution time. Surfaces the highest-risk conversations at the top of the queue.

### 56. Delivery Delay Risk Forecast
Analyses historical delivery performance by zone and courier to calculate delay probability for each active in-transit order. Flags high-risk deliveries before the customer complains. Based on zone-level delay rates, parcel age, and courier performance history.

### 57. Workforce Load Forecast
Analyses message and conversation volumes by day of week and hour of day over the past 60 days to predict the next 7 days of expected workload. Tells managers which days and hours will be busiest so they can schedule agents accordingly.

### 58. Campaign Uplift Forecast
Before launching a campaign, enter the target segment, discount %, and estimated campaign cost. The AI calculates the expected increase in orders and revenue based on historical campaign uplift data. Computes expected ROI. Removes the "will this campaign pay off?" uncertainty.

### 59. Model Accuracy Tracking (MAPE / WMAPE)
Every forecast model is evaluated weekly against actual outcomes. Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE) and Weighted MAPE are calculated per product and stored historically. Merchants can see how accurate the AI predictions have been and watch accuracy improve over time.

### 60. What-If Scenario Simulator
Interactive simulator with four scenario types:
- **Demand / Lead Time:** "What if my supplier's lead time increases from 3 days to 7 days? How much extra safety stock do I need?"
- **Cash Flow:** "What if I cut expenses by 2,000 SAR/day? How does my runway change?"
- **Campaign:** "What will my revenue look like if I run a 20% discount to my top-spending segment?"
- **Pricing:** "If I raise prices by 10%, how does revenue change given expected volume elasticity?"
Results are instant and can be saved as named scenarios for comparison.

## SECTION 8: FINANCE & SUBSCRIPTIONS

### 61. Subscription & Plan Management
Merchants subscribe to Tash8eel on monthly or annual plans. Plans are tiered (Starter, Growth, Pro, Enterprise) with different feature gates. Upgrade and downgrade flows. Grace period handling. Arabic-language billing summaries. Integration with Stripe for card payments and support for bank transfer invoicing.

### 62. Subscription Usage Monitor
Real-time dashboard showing how much of each subscription limit is being consumed: messages sent, orders processed, AI calls used, team seats active. Usage bars with colour-coded warnings at 80% and 90% of plan limits. Prevents surprise overages.

### 63. Add-on & Feature Store
Merchants can purchase individual feature add-ons without upgrading their entire plan: extra AI message credits, additional team seats, advanced reports, extra broadcast quota. Self-service with immediate activation.

### 64. Invoice & Billing History
Full billing history with downloadable PDF invoices. Arabic invoice format with VAT line items. Payment method management. Failed payment retry logic with automated WhatsApp reminders to the merchant's own account.

## SECTION 9: TEAM & ACCESS MANAGEMENT

### 65. Multi-Role Team Management
Invite staff members with granular role-based access control. Five roles: Owner, Admin, Manager, Agent, Viewer. Each role has specific permissions enforced at both UI and API level. Audit log of who did what, when.

### 66. Staff Performance Dashboard
Compare performance metrics across team members: conversations handled, average response time, orders converted, escalation rate, and login activity. Identify top performers and those who need coaching.

### 67. Session & API Key Management
Merchants and staff have separately scoped API keys. Keys can be rotated, revoked, and scoped to specific operations. Every API call is logged with the key that made it. Rate limiting per key. Supports machine-to-machine integration scenarios.

## SECTION 10: INTEGRATIONS & INFRASTRUCTURE

### 68. POS Integration Hub
Connect Tash8eel to popular Arabic-market point-of-sale systems. Sync products and stock levels bidirectionally. Map POS product codes to Tash8eel SKUs. Conflict resolution for stock discrepancies. Webhook-based real-time sync.

### 69. Webhook & Event Streaming
Emit structured JSON events for all key business actions: order created, payment confirmed, message received, stock low, etc. External systems (ERPs, data warehouses, custom apps) can subscribe to these webhooks. Full retry logic with exponential backoff and delivery log.

### 70. Import / Export
Bulk import products, customers, or orders via CSV. Full data export for all entities with configurable field selection. Supports UTF-8 Arabic encoding. Scheduled automatic exports to email or SFTP. Used for migrations, backups, and accountant handoffs.

### 71. WhatsApp Business API Integration
Deep integration with the WhatsApp Business API (Meta Cloud API). Manages session state, message delivery, template approval, quality rating monitoring, and opt-out compliance. Handles all 24-hour session window rules automatically.

### 72. Audit Log
Immutable log of every significant action in the system: who created/edited/deleted what, and when. Filterable by user, action type, date range, and entity. Tamper-evident design. Required for regulatory compliance in financial services contexts.

## SECTION 12: AI VOICE CALLING ENGINE

### 81. AI Outbound Sales Call Agent
The AI places outbound phone calls to leads and customers on behalf of the merchant, speaking in natural Arabic. It introduces the merchant's brand, presents products or offers, handles basic objections, and attempts to convert the call to an order. Calls are fully scripted but dynamically adapted based on the customer's responses. Supports Gulf Arabic dialects.

### 82. AI Inbound Call Handler
When a customer calls the merchant's business number, the AI answers, greets them in Arabic, and handles common requests: checking order status, getting product information, placing a new order, or being routed to a human agent. No hold music, no missed calls — 24/7 automated inbound handling.

### 83. AI Collections Call Agent
For overdue or unpaid orders, the AI places a polite Arabic collection call to the customer. It references the specific order, states the amount due, offers payment options, and captures a verbal commitment to pay. Call outcomes (promised, refused, wrong number, no answer) are logged automatically. Escalates to human agent after failed attempts.

### 84. Order Confirmation Call
After a new order is placed via WhatsApp, the AI automatically calls the customer to verbally confirm the order details — items, quantities, delivery address, and total. Reduces cancellation rates and address errors. Merchant can configure which order types trigger a confirmation call (e.g., orders above a threshold amount).

### 85. Delivery Notification Call
When an order is out for delivery or the driver is nearby, the AI calls the customer to alert them and confirm someone will be available to receive the order. Reduces failed delivery attempts and redelivery costs.

### 86. Win-Back Call Campaign
The AI calls lapsed customers (configurable inactivity window) with a personalised message referencing their last purchase and presenting a comeback offer. More effective than text alone for high-value customers. Call results feed into the CRM — customers who express interest are flagged for human follow-up.

### 87. Post-Delivery Satisfaction Call
After an order is marked delivered, the AI calls the customer to ask a simple satisfaction question ("هل وصلك طلبك بشكل صحيح؟"). Captures yes/no/issue responses. Issues trigger an immediate escalation alert to the merchant's support team. Creates a lightweight CSAT data stream with zero manual effort.

### 88. Call Recording & Transcription
Every AI and human call is recorded (with compliance disclosure at call start) and automatically transcribed to Arabic text. Transcripts are linked to the customer profile and order record. Searchable full-text. Merchants can replay, review, and use transcripts for dispute resolution or quality coaching.

### 89. Call Analytics Dashboard
Track all call activity: total calls made, answer rate, average call duration, outcome breakdown (order placed, payment promised, issue logged, no answer, refused). Trend charts over time. Per-agent breakdown for human calls. Identifies which call types deliver the highest conversion.

### 90. Hybrid Call + WhatsApp Flows
Seamlessly combine voice and messaging in a single customer journey. Example: AI sends a WhatsApp message first, and if unanswered after X hours, automatically escalates to a call. Or: after a successful call, the AI sends a WhatsApp follow-up with the order summary or payment link. Full cross-channel conversation threading.

### 91. Call Schedule & Calling Hours Control
Merchants configure calling windows (e.g., only call 9am–9pm), blocked days (Fridays, national holidays), and maximum call attempts per customer per day. Fully compliant with local telecommunications regulations. Respects customer opt-out of calls while preserving WhatsApp messaging.

### 92. Human Agent Call Transfer
When the AI cannot resolve a call (complex complaint, angry customer, large order negotiation), it performs a warm handover to a human agent with a spoken summary: "مرحباً، أنا أحول لك المحادثة مع العميل أحمد بخصوص طلب رقم 1042." The human agent receives a live call with context pre-loaded on their screen.

## SECTION 13: AI INTELLIGENCE LAYER (CROSS-CUTTING)

### 77. Cancellation Risk Badge
On the orders page, each order with a high likelihood of cancellation (based on heuristics: payment method, time since order, customer history, message sentiment) is flagged with a colour-coded risk badge. Helps agents prioritise orders that need proactive intervention.

### 78. AI Product Description Generator
One click from the inventory page generates a polished Arabic product description for any item, based on the product name, category, price, and any notes the merchant provides. Uses GPT-4o. Saves hours of copywriting per product.

### 79. Lead Score on Follow-ups
Each follow-up (open sales conversation) is automatically assigned a lead quality score (0–100) based on engagement signals: message frequency, product pages viewed, intent keywords detected, and purchase history. Agents focus on the hottest leads first.

### 80. AI-Powered Demand Forecasting (Basic)
The original basic demand forecast computes average daily sales velocity per product and projects 7-day and 30-day demand from recent order history. Fast and simple. Available on all plans. The advanced forecasting platform (Section 7) extends this with full statistical modelling.
