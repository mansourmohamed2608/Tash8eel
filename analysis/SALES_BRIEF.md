## PART 1 — WHAT EXACTLY ARE WE SELLING

### The Product

**Tash8eel** is a **SaaS platform** that lets any Arabic-speaking business run their entire commercial operation through WhatsApp, powered by AI.

It is not a chatbot. It is not a CRM bolt-on. It is not a generic automation tool translated into Arabic.

It is a **full operating system for Arabic-market businesses** — built from the ground up for WhatsApp, for Arabic dialects, for right-to-left, and for how commerce actually works in the GCC and MENA region (bank transfers, payment proof photos, COD, informal buying flows, and voice note negotiations).

---

### The Single-Sentence Pitch

> **"Tash8eel turns your WhatsApp number into a fully automated business — taking orders, chasing payments, managing stock, forecasting demand, and reporting to you every morning — all in Arabic, all without hiring extra staff."**

---

### What You Actually Get When You Subscribe

There are **four capability layers** included in every plan:

#### Layer 1 — The AI Agent (replaces your WhatsApp sales staff)
An AI that lives inside your WhatsApp number and handles all incoming messages 24/7. It:
- Reads customer messages in Arabic (colloquial dialects, not just formal Arabic)
- Answers product questions from your catalog instantly
- Takes complete orders — items, quantities, address, delivery details
- Sends customers payment instructions or payment links
- Collects payment proof photos when customers transfer money
- Chases unpaid orders automatically with polite follow-up messages
- Escalates emotional or complex situations to a human agent

The AI does not need to sleep. It does not go on holiday. It does not forget to follow up. It handles a 3am order the same as a 3pm order.

#### Layer 2 — The Merchant Portal (your business dashboard)
A full web dashboard at `portal.tash8eel.com` where you see and manage everything:
- All conversations and orders in real time
- Customer profiles with full purchase history
- Inventory levels across products and locations
- Delivery tracking and driver assignment
- Team management and agent performance
- Analytics, forecasting, and AI insights

Everything is in Arabic, RTL layout, Arabic number/date formatting. It works on desktop and mobile.

#### Layer 3 — Automation Engine (runs your business while you sleep)
A configurable set of automations that fire without manual intervention:
- Low stock alert → WhatsApp message to you
- Unpaid order → polite Arabic reminder to customer (stops automatically when paid)
- Order delivered → follow-up upsell message 48 hours later
- Customer inactive 21 days → win-back campaign
- Morning summary report → delivered to your WhatsApp every day at 8am
- Conversation not answered in X hours → internal SLA alert to manager

Merchants configure all of this from a self-service control center. No code required.

#### Layer 4 — AI Intelligence & Forecasting (tells you what will happen before it happens)
- Demand forecast per product: how much will sell in the next 7/14/30 days
- Stockout date prediction: when exactly will each product run out
- Cash flow runway: how many days of cash do you have left
- Customer churn probability: which customers are about to stop buying
- Inventory valuation (FIFO): true cost of goods sold
- What-if simulator: "what if I raise prices 10%? What if supplier lead time doubles?"
- Daily AI brief delivered to your WhatsApp every morning with commentary, not just numbers

## PART 3 — HOW IT HELPS (THE REAL PROBLEMS WE SOLVE)

### Problem 1 — WhatsApp is chaos at scale

Every business starts selling on WhatsApp manually. One person reads all messages, types replies, confirms orders in their head, sends payment details, then copies it all into a notebook or spreadsheet. This works until you get more than ~20 messages a day. Then orders fall through the cracks, customers get angry, and the owner can never take a day off.

**What Tash8eel does:** The AI handles all incoming messages automatically. Orders are structured and tracked. Nothing falls through. The owner sees a clean dashboard instead of an unread WhatsApp thread.

---

### Problem 2 — No staff to handle 24/7 inquiries

Customers message at 11pm on Friday. No one replies until Sunday. The customer already ordered from a competitor.

**What Tash8eel does:** The AI answers instantly at any hour, any day, including weekends and national holidays. It takes the order, confirms details, sends payment instructions — the whole flow — without a human involved.

---

### Problem 3 — Unpaid orders pile up

Many merchants in the MENA region use bank transfer as the primary payment method. Customers "forget" to pay, or pay and don't send proof, or send proof and the merchant loses track. Chasing payments manually is embarrassing and time-consuming.

**What Tash8eel does:** The AI sends polite follow-up messages automatically on a set schedule. When the customer sends a payment screenshot, the system's OCR reads it, extracts the amount and date, and the merchant approves it with one click.

---

### Problem 4 — Stock surprises

The merchant runs out of a product mid-campaign, or over-orders on something that doesn't sell. Both situations cost real money.

**What Tash8eel does:** Real-time inventory tracking with automatic low-stock WhatsApp alerts. The forecasting engine predicts stockout dates per product. Safety stock and reorder point calculations tell the merchant exactly when and how much to order.

---

### Problem 5 — No visibility into the business

Most small business owners have no idea what their true revenue is, which products are actually profitable, or which customers are about to churn. They run on gut feel.

**What Tash8eel does:** Daily Arabic WhatsApp brief every morning. Full analytics dashboard. CFO-grade monthly reports. Customer churn scores. Conversion funnel analysis. All in Arabic, all automatically generated.

### WhatsApp-Only AI Pricing Method (Quick Quote)

Use this method when a merchant only needs AI replies on WhatsApp (no inventory, no multi-branch, no advanced ops modules).

Formula:

`Monthly Price = Base WhatsApp AI Fee + Extra AI Action Cost + WhatsApp/BSP Pass-Through + Support Margin`

Where:

`AI actions/month = daily conversations x 30 x AI actions per conversation`

Recommended sales defaults (Egypt):
- Base WhatsApp AI fee: **2,500 EGP/mo**
- Included AI actions: **10,000 actions/mo**
- Extra usage: **120 EGP per 1,000 actions**

Example (500 conversations/day):
- Light-medium automation (2 actions/conversation):
	- AI actions/month = `500 x 30 x 2 = 30,000`
	- Extra actions = `30,000 - 10,000 = 20,000`
	- Extra cost = `20 x 120 = 2,400 EGP`
	- Subtotal before BSP pass-through/margin = `2,500 + 2,400 = 4,900 EGP/mo`
- Heavier automation (3 actions/conversation):
	- AI actions/month = `500 x 30 x 3 = 45,000`
	- Extra actions = `35,000`
	- Extra cost = `35 x 120 = 4,200 EGP`
	- Subtotal before BSP pass-through/margin = `2,500 + 4,200 = 6,700 EGP/mo`

Commercial quoting guidance:
- Safe quote range for this profile: **4,900 to 6,700 EGP/mo** before adding any special SLA or premium support.
- If the customer wants a fixed number, offer a capped plan around **6,500 EGP/mo** with clear usage thresholds.

### GPT-4o-mini Token Cost Reference (for internal pricing)

Use this only as an internal cost input, not as the customer-facing line item.

Token cost formula:

`LLM cost = (input_tokens/1,000,000 x input_rate) + (output_tokens/1,000,000 x output_rate)`

Working assumptions used in estimates:
- Model: **gpt-4o-mini**
- Input rate: **$0.15 / 1M tokens**
- Output rate: **$0.60 / 1M tokens**
- Typical per AI reply: **300-800 input tokens** and **80-250 output tokens**

Example for 500 conversations/day at 2 AI replies per conversation:
- Replies/day = `500 x 2 = 1,000`
- Replies/month = `1,000 x 30 = 30,000`
- Input tokens/month = `9M to 24M`
- Output tokens/month = `2.4M to 7.5M`
- Estimated monthly LLM bill = **$2.79 to $8.10**

At 3 AI replies per conversation, multiply this range by 1.5.

Note: This excludes WhatsApp conversation fees, hosting, OCR/maps/voice services, onboarding/support time, and margin.

## PART 7 — FREQUENTLY ASKED SALES QUESTIONS

**Q: Do customers need to download anything?**
No. Customers just use WhatsApp as they always do. Nothing changes on their end.

**Q: Does the merchant need to install anything?**
No. The merchant accesses the dashboard from any browser. Setup is done by the Tash8eel team — the merchant connects their WhatsApp Business number and it goes live.

**Q: What if the AI says something wrong?**
The AI only answers from the merchant's own product catalog and configured rules. It does not make up information. If it cannot answer, it escalates to a human agent. The merchant can review and correct any AI response.

**Q: How long does it take to set up?**
For standard businesses, onboarding is typically same-day to 72 hours. We import the product catalog, configure automations, and go live.

**Q: Is there a contract?**
No long-term contract is required. Month-to-month billing is available on all plans. Annual plans exist for customers who want the discount.

**Q: What's included in "Platform Core"?**
Hosting on cloud infrastructure, security (SOC2-aligned, end-to-end encrypted), and customer support. This is included in every plan.

**Q: Is it in Arabic?**
The merchant portal is fully Arabic with RTL layout. The AI responds in Arabic. All system messages, reports, and alerts are in Arabic. This is not a translated product — it was designed in Arabic first.

| **محادثات الواتساب** | إجمالي عدد محادثات الواتساب اللي بتتفتح في الشهر — كل عميل جديد بيبدأ محادثة = واحدة من الرصيد. |
