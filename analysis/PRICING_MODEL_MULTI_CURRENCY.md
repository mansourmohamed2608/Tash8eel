## COMMERCIAL GUARDRAILS & POLICIES

### Hard Usage Limits (automatic upgrade trigger)
- At 120% of included quota, customer receives overage alert.
- At 150% of included quota, plan is flagged for mandatory upgrade or overage negotiation.
- No merchant allowed to exceed 150% in production without explicit add-on or plan upgrade.

### Free Trial & Early-Adopter Incentives (optional)
- **First 30 days:** 50% off (Starter and Basic only).
- **Annual prepay discount:** 15% off monthly price (all plans).
- **Upgrade credit:** If merchant upgrades within 60 days, credit 50% of Starter overpayment toward new plan.

### SAP Integration & Invoicing
- **Invoice frequency:** Monthly or annual.
- **Invoice format:** Arabic + English bilingual, with line-item breakdown of usage.
- **Payment terms:** Net 5 days for monthly / Net 15 days for annual.
- **Auto-on-fail:** Failed payment triggers automated WhatsApp reminder on merchant's account.

## REGIONAL STRATEGY NOTES

### Egypt Market
- Price sensitivity is high; affordability multiplier (0.78×) reflects market realities.
- Expect high volume; margin protection relies on operational efficiency, not pricing power.
- Consider **freemium** or **credit-based trial** to drive adoption.

### Saudi Arabia
- Strong market with professional merchant base.
- Multiplier (1.05×) is near-USD; Saudi merchants expect Western-quality features.
- Opportunity to upsell to **Professional** tier early if merchant shows demand signals.

### UAE & Kuwait
- Premium markets with high willingness-to-pay.
- Multipliers (1.12× AED, 1.08× KWD) reflect affluence and strong digital infrastructure.
- Focus on **enterprise relationships**; support-hour monetization is highly viable.

### Oman
- Emerging market; multiplier (1.0×) treats as base affordability.
- Growth potential but lower current ARPU.
- Monitor churn closely; price elasticity may be high.

## ASSUMPTIONS & SENSITIVITY

### Key Model Assumptions
- **AI action volume:** 1,000–5,000/month per tier (drives 20–30% of variable cost).
- **WhatsApp template cost blend:** 55% utility, 25% service, 15% marketing, 5% authentication.
- **Enterprise support:** Capped at 8 hours/month included; additional billed at $120/hour or $160/hour (dedicated).
- **Utilization:** Expected usage is 65% of quota; full usage is 100%; conservative is 35%.

### If AI Costs Rise
- **+10% AI cost:** Margin drops by **0.4–0.7 percentage points** (manageable).
- **+20% AI cost:** Margin drops by **0.8–1.4 percentage points** (increase price or reduce AI inclusions).
- **+30% AI cost:** Margin drops by **1.2–2.1 percentage points** (implement immediate price increase or cap AI usage).

**Recommendation:** If AI costs rise >15% sustained, trigger Emergency Price Review and consider moving AI actions to strict add-on model.

## SPREADSHEET-READY FORMULAS

### Monthly subscription revenue (USD):
```
=B2 * COUNT(active_merchants_on_plan) * 12 / 12
```

### Currency conversion (any plan, any currency):
```
=USD_base_price * affordability_multiplier * FX_rate_to_usd
```

### Overage revenue (monthly):
```
=MAX(actual_usage - included_quota, 0) * overage_unit_price
```

### Gross margin:
```
=(Monthly_Price - total_COGS) / Monthly_Price
```

### Annual billing incentive (15% off):
```
=Monthly_Price * 12 * 0.85
```
