## FINANCIAL GUARDRAILS

### When to Raise or Lower Price

**Raise price if:**
- Gross margin drops below 72% for 2 consecutive quarters.
- Utilization of included quotas exceeds 90% on average per tier.
- Competitor pricing increases by >10%.
- AI cost inflation sustained >15%.

**Lower price if:**
- Churn rate exceeds 8% per month on any tier.
- CAC payback period exceeds 18 months.
- Market data shows affordability pressure in Egypt/Oman.

**Review quarterly** with Finance + Product + Sales.
